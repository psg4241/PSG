C.Write a MongoDB query to insert, query, update and delete a document. 
 
Query: 
 
> db.mycol.insert([{title:"MongoDB Tutorial",description:"MongoDB is no sql database",by:"Tutorials Point",tags:["mongodb","database","NoSQL"],likes:100}]) 
 
 
> db.mycol.find().pretty() 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
> db.mycol.update({title:"MongoDB Tutorial"},{$set:{title:"MongoDB Overview"}}) 
 
 
> db.mycol.find().pretty() 
 
 
> db.mycol.remove({title:"MongoDB Overview"}) > db.mycol.find().pretty()
